Mahendra Singh Dhoni

The cricket legend Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni was born in Ranchi, Bihar (now in Jharkhand) and he hails from a Hindu Rajput family.

He is a former Indian international right-handed middle-order batsman and wicket-keeper

who captained the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014.

Under his captaincy, India won the inaugural 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.

Dhoni is one of the highest run scorers in One Day Internationals (ODIs) with more than 10,000 runs scored and is considered an effective "finisher" in limited-overs formats.

He is widely regarded as one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen and captains in the history of the game

MS Dhoni announced his retirement from international cricket on 15 August 2020
